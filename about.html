Background

I've not always wanted to be an electronic engineer or even more tenuously a radioglaciologist, although it is perhaps easy to see how a LEGO childhood, trips to the post-glacial landscapes of the UK's national parks and skiing holidays in the Alps might have something to do with it.

It wasn't until a second trip to the Mer-de-Glace glacier in Chamonix, seeing the extent of just one year's melt with my own eyes, that I really began to look for ways to shoehorn electronics and the Alpine world into a career path ahead of me. The RGS IBG Explore conference later that year opened my eyes to the world of polar science and I was intruiged.
